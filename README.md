# Sales Performance Analysis (Excel)

## Overview
This project analyzes sales data to identify revenue trends, 
high and low-performing product categories, and the impact of 
payment methods on sales using Microsoft Excel.

## Business Objective
To help stakeholders understand which product categories generate 
the least revenue and how sales change over time.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Charts)

## Key Insights
- Clothing and Books have high units sold but low revenue due to low pricing
- Electronics generate the highest revenue despite lower sales volume
- Sales show an overall declining trend after March
- Payment method does not significantly impact sales performance

## Deliverables
- PDF report containing analysis, charts, and conclusions
